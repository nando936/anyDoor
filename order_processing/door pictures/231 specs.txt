stiles and rails are 3" wide
material is 13/16 thick for stiles and rails
and for the panel it is whatever is called out in the order for the panel

this is a mitre cut door so stile and rail length calculations are exact outside dimensions of the door

sticking for this style is 1/2 

panel sizes for the door will be finish sizes - width + (sticking - 1/8) for both width and height

this is a 5 piece door
a 5 piece door has 5 parts it has 
2 stiles
2 rails
1 panel
